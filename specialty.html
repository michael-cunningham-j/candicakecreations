When You Want Something Different

Specialty creations are our favorite kind of challenge. These are the sculpted, themed, and just-plain-fun cakes that go beyond the traditional tier. Think hobby-inspired designs, holiday sculpts, character cakes, and edible showpieces made from scratch. Bring us the idea and we'll bring it to life in buttercream and fondant.

How a Specialty Cake Comes Together

1. Share Your Vision

Send a photo, sketch, or description of what you'd like. Include the event date, servings needed, and any flavor or dietary preferences.

2. Design & Quote

Candi will follow up with a design plan, timeline, and price. Specialty sculpts require more lead time, so please reach out early.

3. Baked to Order

Once approved, your cake is baked, sculpted, and finished from scratch. Delivery and curbside pickup available in the Cedar Rapids area.
