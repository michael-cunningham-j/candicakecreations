About Candi

Candi's Cake Creations is a home-based bakery in Cedar Rapids, Iowa specializing in creating cakes, cupcakes, and cake balls for every kind of celebration.

Every cake is baked from scratch and custom-designed to match your vision. Whether you need an elegant wedding cake, a whimsical birthday centerpiece, or a batch of cupcakes for the office, we'd love to bake it for you.

Delivery and curbside pickup available.
